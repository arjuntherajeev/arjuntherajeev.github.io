Philosophy

As a hobbyist photographer, I aspire to make you look at my photos and pause to reflect. I believe that all kinds of photography bring forth a unique set of emotions in the viewer. My goal is to help you invoke these emotions at ease and to feed your soul with the beauty of everything around us.

Interests

I don't have a preferred genre of photography but my interests revolve around landscapes, cityscapes, and skyscapes. I am particularly fond of photographing sunrises and sunsets. You will also find photos of nature, especially intricate shots of flora and foliage scattered in my collection.
